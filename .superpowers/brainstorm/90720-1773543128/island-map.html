Island Map Layout — Where does each area live?

World is ~160×100 tiles (5120×3200px). Camera pans to whoever is active. Pick the geography.

A — Linear Coastline

All 4 areas arranged left→right along a single beach. Camera slides horizontally as bottle travels. Simple, predictable, fast to pan. Bottle route is a straight horizontal path.

Widest map. Pan direction always matches task direction (left = start, right = done).

B — Round Island, Winding Path

Classic AC oval island shape. Areas at compass-ish points: Town Hall NW, Forest NE, Library E (dock into sea), Café S. Bottle path winds organically. Camera pans diagonally.

Most like real Animal Crossing. Feels like a place, not a conveyor belt. Best exploration potential.

C — Connected Districts

4 distinct island chunks connected by stone bridges/paths through a central plaza. Each district has its own biome. Camera jumps between districts as bottle travels. Most modular.

Easiest to extend — add new villager = add new island chunk. Very clear visual ownership per villager.
