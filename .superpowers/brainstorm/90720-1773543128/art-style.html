A — Chunky Pixel Art

16×16 tiles, sprite-sheet style characters. Classic Game Boy Advance energy. Fast to draw, instantly readable, very Animal Crossing GCN era.

FLAT VECTOR · SMOOTH

SVG-style flat characters drawn with Canvas arcs and rects. Smooth, modern, cozy. Think Animal Crossing New Horizons UI energy. No sprite sheets needed.

8px DITHER · GAME BOY VIBE

C — Dithered Retro

8×8 pixel grid with dithered shading. Strong Game Boy / Pokémon-era nostalgia. Extremely fast to render — just setPixel calls. Distinctly handcrafted.

Canvas: 8px tiles · 4-color palette per zone · 60fps on any hardware
